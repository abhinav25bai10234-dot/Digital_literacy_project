**Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist**

**Do's**

1. Do keep your privacy settings updated to control who sees your personal posts and location.

2. Do use professional platforms like LinkedIn to showcase your HackerRank certifications and projects.

3. Do verify the source of information or news before sharing it to prevent the spread of misinformation

4. Do use a professional profile picture and a clean bio on platforms where you interact with faculty or recruiters.

5. Do follow and engage with industry leaders and organizations related to your branch of study.

**Don'ts**

1.Don't post offensive or inflammatory comments that could negatively impact your future career or "Digital Footprint".

2.Don't share sensitive information like your registration number, OTPs, or home address publicly.

3.Don't engage with or "troll" others in online arguments; maintain a respectful tone at all times.

4.Don't tag others in photos or posts without their explicit permission.

5.Don't click on suspicious "clickbait" links or ads that often lead to phishing sites.
