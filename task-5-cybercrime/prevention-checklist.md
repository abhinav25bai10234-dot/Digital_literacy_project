**Stay Safe Online: Essential Tips for Students**


**Enable MFA: **Always use Multi-Factor Authentication for email and banking.


**Verify UPI Requests:** Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money; a PIN is only required to send money.


**Check the URL:** Look for https:// and double-check domain names for typos (e.g., v1tbhopal instead of vitbhopal).


**Official Apps Only**: Only use official banking or payment apps from the Play Store/App Store.


**Privacy Settings**: Keep social media profiles private to prevent identity harvesters from gathering personal details.


**Public Wi-Fi Warning**: Avoid logging into bank accounts while using free public Wi-Fi.


**Secure Passwords:** Use unique passphrases for different accounts rather than one master password.


**Report Immediately:** If scammed, immediately call the National Cyber Crime Helpline at 1930 or report at cybercrime.gov.in
